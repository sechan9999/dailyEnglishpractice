Overfitting
/ˌoʊvərˈfɪtɪŋ/
🇰🇷 과적합
Model learns training data noise and fails on new data.
Bias
/ˈbaɪ.əs/
🇰🇷 편향
Error from overly simplified model assumptions.
Variance
/ˈveər.i.əns/
🇰🇷 분산
Sensitivity to small fluctuations in training data.
Generalization
/ˌdʒen.ər.əl.aɪˈzeɪ.ʃən/
🇰🇷 일반화
Model ability to perform well on unseen data.
